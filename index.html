Money Left — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Money Left mobile app (package com.money.left, the “App”), provided by Vambran Studio (“we”, “us”), handles information. Money Left helps you track your monthly budget — how much money is left after expenses, a daily spending limit, and your day-to-day spending. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Summary

  • The App has no account or sign-in. The budget data you enter stays on your device.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Third-party services we use (ads, the app stores) collect limited device and usage data under their own policies.

Information you provide

You can enter income sources, recurring expenses, one-off transactions (spends/earnings), currency preferences, and app settings. This information is stored locally on your device (in the App’s on-device database). It is not transmitted to or stored on our servers, and we have no way to access it. It is removed when you delete the relevant item in the App or uninstall the App.

Information collected automatically

  • Advertising data. The App shows rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect your device’s advertising identifier and device/usage information. See Google’s Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps that use its services.
  • Purchases. The optional “Pro” upgrade is processed by Google Play Billing (Android) or Apple StoreKit / App Store (iOS). Those platforms handle the transaction; we do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Diagnostics. Google Play services and the ads SDK may collect aggregate, non-identifying app-performance and crash data per the links above.

Network requests

  • Currency exchange rates. To convert and display amounts in your chosen currencies, the App fetches up-to-date fiat and crypto exchange rates from a third-party rates provider. These requests ask only for rate data — none of your financial data, amounts, or personal information is sent.
  • Accurate time. The App queries public network time (NTP) servers to keep daily budgets accurate. No personal data is sent.

How we use information

On-device data is used only to provide the App’s features (calculations, charts, daily limits). Advertising and store data are used by Google/Apple to deliver ads, process purchases, and keep their services secure and working, as described in their policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We do not share the budget data you enter with anyone. The only data sharing is with the service providers named above (Google AdMob, Google Play / Apple), strictly to provide their part of the App.

Data retention and deletion

Because your budget data lives only on your device, you control it directly: delete individual incomes, expenses, or transactions in the App, or uninstall the App to remove all of it. To reset advertising data, use your device’s “Reset advertising ID” / “Reset Identifier for Advertisers” setting.

Children’s privacy

Money Left is a general-audience budgeting app and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will address it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data and to object to certain processing. Since the App stores your budget data only on your device and we hold no personal data about you, you can exercise these directly through the App and your device settings. For ad-related choices, Google may present a consent prompt where required, and you can manage ad personalization in your device settings.

Security

Your budget data remains on your device and is protected by your device’s own security (lock screen, OS sandboxing). Network requests use secure (HTTPS) connections. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact us at vambranstudio@gmail.com.
